Recreation

Welcome to 10-Star Hotel recreational services.
We offer different kinds of recreational services.
We have:

Archery

For the love of aiming and shooting. Archery is the game of accuracy and perfection. Come and enjoy the skills of accuracy by shooting your best shot.

Chess

A master mind game. Chess will help you boost and jog your mind. Enter the world's leadersboard by playing the best chess games in our Hotel. Best for Kings and Queens love..

Golf

Just a single swing of the golf stick will relief your mind. Golf helps you relax your muscles as you swing and jog around. That's why we offer the best service.

Gymnastics

We have a world class gymnasium room with all kind of exercise equipments. With full time trainers you get to enjoy aerobics and other physical fitness exercises.

We also have multiple swimmming pools for different activities such as world competitions and personal recreation purposes (Adults and children separately).

From freezing countries? You love snow? We got you covered, special freezing environments for snow skiing and alot of water for water skiing.

We love water, everyone loves water, that is why we have boat racing in our hotel to let you compete and see wo is the world's champion.

Lovers of football and video gaming, come and show the world your skills in dodging and dribbling around. We also have virtual and augmented reality games.

We have tour guides who are competent enough to take you through a walk in the park visit us to know the beauty of our nation through safari tours.

Line Riding

Enjoy the fun of line riding that we offer at 10-Star, roll and rock with the joy of being "on-air" just like a pilot. Come and let us enjoy together
